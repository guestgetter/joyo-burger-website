PROUDLY SERVING ROSEMONT

Located in the vibrant Rosemont-La Petite-Patrie borough, JOYO Burger Masson is proud to be part of this diverse and dynamic Montreal neighborhood. Just steps away from the beautiful Parc Maisonneuve and the Montreal Botanical Garden, our restaurant serves both locals and visitors seeking authentic, quality halal burgers.

Getting Here

We're easily accessible via public transportation:

  • Bus lines #31 and #47 stop nearby on Masson Street
  • Joliette metro station (Green Line) is a 12-minute walk
  • Street parking is available on Masson Street and surrounding areas

Nearby Attractions

Make JOYO Burger part of your day exploring Rosemont:

  • Parc Maisonneuve (15-minute walk)
  • Montreal Botanical Garden (20-minute walk)
  • Cinéma Beaubien (8-minute walk)
  • Promenade Masson shopping district (we're right in it!)
